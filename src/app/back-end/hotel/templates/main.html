    About Our Company
    How did we came to this idea?

    The idea for Jhoola was born as an innovative solution to avoid conflicts between tourists when booking sunbeds, while at the same time facilitating the work of receptionists in this area. We realised that competition for a comfortable place to relax, such as a hammock by the pool, could lead to tensions between visitors and complications for the booking staff. We therefore set out to develop a platform that would simplify this process and promote a more harmonious experience for all involved.

    With Jhoola, users can easily book their desired hammock in advance, avoiding disputes and ensuring a relaxing experience from the moment they arrive at the destination.

    Our idea is to offer a new service that is rarely seen on the market in our continent.

    We want there to be a new connection between hoteliers and tourists and to pioneer a new economy that will benefit us all.
